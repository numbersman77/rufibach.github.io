Kaspar Rufibach

Senior Principal Statistical Scientist

Methods, Collaboration, and Outreach Group, Department of Biostatistics, F. Hoffmann-La Roche, Basel, Switzerland

Biography

Kaspar Rufibach has received training as a mathematical statistician. Through more than 15 years in various biostatistical roles he has complemented his thorough theoretical background with expertise in biostatistics. He is passionate about applying this expertise to solve problems in pharmaceutical drug development.

Interests

  • Statistical methods to optimize study designs
  • Advanced survival analysis
  • Probability of success
  • Estimands
  • Nonparametric statistics
